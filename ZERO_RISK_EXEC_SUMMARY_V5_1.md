# Zero-Risk Executive Summary v5.1

Date: March 27, 2026
Audience: Founders, legal counsel, investor and launch stakeholders

## Strategic Outcome

The platform now has a launch-grade compliance architecture that converts legal obligations into enforceable operating controls. This covers legal ownership, statutory SLA execution, moderation governance, incident response, tax/billing controls, and app-store safety.

## Locked Operating Posture

1. India-operated and India-billed global platform.
2. 18+ policy platform-wide at launch.
3. Founder-led interim compliance ownership with deputy and tertiary fallback.
4. Staged rollout: India -> Singapore/Nepal -> US/EU.

## What Is Fully Covered

- India IT Rules clocks (2h/3h/36h/24h/7d), CERT-In 6h, and evidence retention controls.
- Privacy rights, deletion-vs-retention clarity, breach notification workflows.
- DMCA, CSAM, law-enforcement request processing.
- AI/SGI labeling and provenance logging.
- App-store compliance layer with release blocking gate.
- Subscription cancellation, refund, and dispute controls.
- Access controls, break-glass approval, immutable audit trails.
- Abuse prevention and contest integrity hardening.

## Go/No-Go Criteria

Do not launch if any critical gate fails:

1. Legal ownership chain is incomplete.
2. SLA and incident drills fail.
3. App-store checklist has blockers.
4. Billing/tax controls are unapproved.
5. Auditability and legal hold controls fail.

## Residual Risk

Residual risk is startup-normal and primarily tied to edge-case legal interpretation and external disputes, not architecture gaps. Counsel validation remains mandatory before launch.
