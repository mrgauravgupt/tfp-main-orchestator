TFP Agreements

Our platform facilitates "Time for Print" (TFP) collaborations. While we provide the digital model release forms and messaging systems, the TFP Platform is not a party to any contract between creators. Any disputes regarding image usage, editing, or timelines must be resolved between the involved parties directly.

Prohibited Conduct

You agree not to upload stolen imagery, solicit unauthorized paid work under the guise of TFP, or harass other members of the community.

Community Guidelines

1. Respect the Craft

TFP collaborations rely on mutual respect. Whether you are a seasoned professional or a beginner, treat everyone's time and effort with dignity. Deliver your edits on time, and communicate clearly.

2. Safety First

Always prioritize physical safety during photoshoots and events. We strongly encourage meeting in public places for initial collaborations and discussing clear boundaries before the shoot begins.

3. Constructive Feedback

If you choose to critique someone's work, ensure your feedback is constructive, actionable, and kind. Bullying, harassment, or elitism will result in an immediate ban.
